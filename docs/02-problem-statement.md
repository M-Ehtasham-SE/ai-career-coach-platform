# Problem Statement: AI-Powered Career Coach Platform

## The Problem
Job seekers in Pakistan face three critical challenges:

### 1. Uncertain Resume Quality
- No objective way to know if their resume will pass ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems)
- Most resumes get rejected within 6 seconds of human review
- Candidates apply to 100+ companies without knowing what's wrong

### 2. Role Confusion
- Unclear which skills to highlight for specific job roles
- Fresh graduates don't know which role suits them best
- Career switchers struggle to present transferable skills

### 3. Interview Anxiety
- No safe space to practice and receive feedback
- Technical interviews are intimidating
- No objective scoring to measure improvement

---

## Current Solutions (Why They Fail)

### Generic Resume Templates
- One-size-fits-all, no role-specific optimization
- Templates look good but content is mediocre

### Paid Career Coaches
- Expensive (PKR 10,000-50,000 per session)
- Limited availability
- Not scalable

### YouTube Tutorials
- Passive learning, no personalized feedback
- No objective scoring
- Generic advice, not tailored

### Mock Interviews with Friends
- Unreliable feedback (friends are biased)
- No AI-driven scoring
- Not structured

---

## Our Solution
An AI-powered platform that:

### 1. Scores Resumes Objectively
- AI analyzes resumes using 20+ parameters
- Returns score from 0-100
- Provides actionable feedback

### 2. Provides Role-Specific Analysis
- Four roles: Frontend, Backend, Data, UI/UX
- Different scoring criteria per role
- Targeted improvement suggestions

### 3. Generates Interview Questions
- AI generates role-specific questions
- Users submit written answers
- AI provides scoring and feedback

### 4. Tracks Progress Over Time
- Visual dashboard with charts
- Historical data comparison
- Gamification (achievements, milestones)

---

## Target Users

### Persona 1: Fresh Graduate (Ahmed, 22)
- **Background:** Just graduated from FAST-NUCES, CGPA 3.0
- **Goal:** Get first job as a Junior Developer
- **Pain Points:** Doesn't know if resume is good enough, afraid of interviews
- **Needs:** Resume scoring, role guidance, interview practice, confidence boost

### Persona 2: Career Switcher (Sana, 28)
- **Background:** Chemical engineer, self-taught Python
- **Goal:** Transition into Data Analytics
- **Pain Points:** How to present non-CS background effectively?
- **Needs:** Role-specific resume optimization, targeted skill recommendations

### Persona 3: Working Professional (Bilal, 35)
- **Background:** 10 years in IT, currently Senior Developer
- **Goal:** Move to Team Lead/Senior Architect role
- **Pain Points:** Resume is outdated, leadership interviews are different
- **Needs:** Executive-level resume optimization, leadership interview practice
