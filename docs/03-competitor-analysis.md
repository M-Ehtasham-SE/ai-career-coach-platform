# Competitor Analysis

## 1. Rezi.ai

### What They Do
AI resume builder with ATS optimization

### Strengths
- Excellent ATS keyword optimization
- Clean UI/UX
- Multiple templates
- Mobile app available

### Weaknesses
- Expensive ($29/month)
- No interview practice module
- No progress tracking
- Only focuses on resumes, not holistic career coaching
- Not Pakistan-specific

### What We Can Learn
- Keyword optimization is critical
- Clean UI matters more than fancy features
- ATS compatibility is a major selling point

---

## 2. Interviewing.io

### What They Do
Anonymous mock interviews with real engineers

### Strengths
- Real human feedback
- Live coding practice
- Company-specific interview prep
- Top-tier engineers as interviewers

### Weaknesses
- Expensive ($40/session)
- Limited availability (need to book slots)
- No resume optimization
- Not available in Pakistan market
- Can't practice anytime (scheduled sessions)

### What We Can Learn
- Structured interview practice is valuable
- Specific company prep is a good feature for future
- Anonymous practice reduces anxiety

---

## 3. Pramp (by Interviewing.io)

### What They Do
Peer-to-peer mock interviews

### Strengths
- Free
- Peer feedback
- Structured format
- Good community

### Weaknesses
- Quality depends on peer (inconsistent)
- No AI scoring
- Limited to technical interviews only
- No resume or career coaching
- Need to schedule with peers

### What We Can Learn
- Free tier is essential for Pakistan market
- AI can replace inconsistent peer feedback
- Structured format works well
