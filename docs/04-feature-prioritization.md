# Feature Prioritization (MoSCoW Method)

## M - MUST HAVE (Critical - Launch Without These = Failure)

### 1. User Authentication
- **Why:** Without personalization, platform is useless
- **Priority:** CRITICAL
- **Week:** 2

### 2. Resume Upload & Storage
- **Why:** Core feature, without this there's no platform
- **Priority:** CRITICAL
- **Week:** 3

### 3. AI Resume Scoring
- **Why:** Primary value proposition
- **Priority:** CRITICAL
- **Week:** 4

### 4. Job Role Selection
- **Why:** Role-specific targeting is key differentiator
- **Priority:** CRITICAL
- **Week:** 5

---

## S - SHOULD HAVE (Important but can launch without)

### 5. Interview Practice Module
- **Why:** Valuable but secondary to resume scoring
- **Can Wait:** Yes, until Week 6
- **Priority:** HIGH

### 6. Progress Dashboard
- **Why:** Shows value over time
- **Can Wait:** Yes, until Week 7
- **Priority:** MEDIUM-HIGH

---

## C - COULD HAVE (Nice to have, not essential)

### 7. Learning Roadmap Recommendations
- **Why:** Enhances value but not core
- **Can Wait:** Future enhancement
- **Priority:** LOW

### 8. Social Sharing
- **Why:** Share achievements on LinkedIn
- **Can Wait:** Future enhancement
- **Priority:** LOW

---

## W - WON'T HAVE (This Version)

### 9. Mobile App
- **Why:** Web-first approach
- **Future:** Yes, if platform succeeds

### 10. Video Interview Analysis
- **Why:** AI video analysis is complex and expensive
- **Future:** Yes, future AI enhancement

### 11. Company-Specific Interview Prep
- **Why:** Requires company-specific data
- **Future:** Yes, partnership opportunity

### 12. ATS Resume Scanner
- **Why:** Requires enterprise partnerships
- **Future:** Yes, future integration

---

## MVP Definition (End of Week 5)

**Minimum Viable Product MUST include:**
- User can register/login
- Upload a resume PDF
- Select a job role (4 options)
- Get AI scoring (0-100)
- Get actionable feedback (strengths, weaknesses, suggestions)
- See score history over time

**Everything else is a bonus.**
